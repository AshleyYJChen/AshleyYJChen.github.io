Background

This program was created to tackle the issues students face at UCSD when preparing for internships and getting the industry level skillset required for working. Enabling access for UX interested students in gaining vital skills along with a Design Lab mindset and real life experience will increase the chances of success for students overall in finding jobs, not just the ones who "get lucky".




Progress

Our team has completed interview and survey questions, stakeholder recruitment, engagement guides, and service design guideline prototyping amoung other work through multiple meeting sessions. These initiatives were done to undestand who to involve and to set up the logistics for conduct. Secondary research and analysis were used to inform us of existing programs and the issues to solve. All this preparation is for the kickoff of the intern project program we are planning to be apart of.
